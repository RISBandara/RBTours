About RB Tours

RB Tours is your premier provider of Wilpattu National Park safari experiences and transportation services in Sri Lanka. With years of experience in the tourism industry, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional service and unforgettable wildlife encounters.

Our team of expert guides and drivers are knowledgeable about Wilpattu's diverse ecosystem and will ensure you have the best possible chance to spot leopards, elephants, sloth bears, and many other species in their natural habitat.

We offer customized safari packages to suit all types of travelers, from solo adventurers to large groups and families.
